BegoneCIA (iOS 11, 12 and 13)
Privacy at your fingertips.

Control Your Privacy

BegoneCIA lets you control your privacy by giving you a CC toggle to enable or disable your microphone, camera and GPS.

Customize It Your Way

You can choose which apps you to block with BegoneCIA and which apps not to, easily select the desired applications from the preference pane in the settings of BegoneCIA.

Known Issues

The microphone might still work in certain applications. I may or may not work on that issue since I didn’t intend on maintaining BegoneCIA besides updating it for arm64e devices and iOS 13. This tweak belongs to NepetaDev, I simply updated it for other devices and iOS 13.

iOS 9 and 10

For devices on iOS 9 - 10.x, please download BegoneCIA (iOS 10 and 9) from Nepeta’s mirror repository I’ve created. You can visit and add it to your package manager at:

https://alexpng.github.io/Nepeta-Mirror
